Home-Schooled Student

Name: Hannah
Representative Quote: “I’m still learning English. My mother got a tutor for me, and I’m happy since it will help me make new friends here.
Biography: She is an only child. Her family moved to America when she was 5 years old. Now, She lives at home in the United States with her parents who homeschool her. Her family’s native language is French which she is most fluent in. She knows very little English and wants to learn more. She doesn’t have many friends at her new home in the United States. She thinks that learning more English might help her find more friends.
Demographic Data: 7, Female
Technical Background: Homeschooled student
Goals for System: She wants to learn more English so that she can find new friends here in America.
